1. Information We Collect

Account and contact information

We may collect your name, business name, email address, login identifiers, billing status, plan information, support messages, and other information you provide when creating or managing an account.

Business, store, and product information

When you onboard a business or connect a store, we may collect business descriptions, website URLs, brand preferences, products, collections, categories, prices, inventory status, product images, policies, shipping or return information, store metadata, and related catalog data.

Customer interaction data

If you use NuMeAi’s chat, widget, or AI sales assistant, we may process chat questions, responses, product clicks, checkout clicks, support requests, session data, and performance events. You are responsible for providing any legally required notices to your own customers regarding your use of NuMeAi on your website.

Social channel and advertising information

If you connect social accounts or ad-related services, we may process account identifiers, page or profile names, access tokens, refresh tokens, permissions/scopes granted, post drafts, scheduled posts, publishing status, media URLs, campaign settings, audience suggestions, and related analytics made available by the connected platform.

AI inputs and outputs

We collect prompts, instructions, store context, competitor inputs, generated reports, chat answers, marketing copy, campaign plans, image-generation requests, and other AI-generated or AI-assisted content.

Usage, device, and log information

We may collect IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, referring URLs, timestamps, API logs, error logs, authentication events, feature usage, and other technical data needed to operate, secure, and improve the Services.

Payment information

Payments and subscriptions may be processed by third-party payment providers. We generally receive billing status, plan, invoice, and transaction metadata, but we do not store full payment card numbers.

2. Sources of Information

We collect information directly from you, from your connected store or website, from connected social or advertising platforms that you authorize, from users who interact with your NuMeAi widget, from service providers, and from publicly available sources where permitted.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Services;
  • Create and manage your account, business profile, SIM, and dashboard;
  • Learn your inventory, categories, pricing, brand voice, and business context;
  • Generate AI chat responses, product recommendations, marketing content, market intelligence reports, and campaign plans;
  • Publish or schedule content to social channels when you authorize us to do so;
  • Support future ad-manager features, including campaign drafts, targeting recommendations, budget suggestions, and approval workflows;
  • Measure usage, improve performance, debug errors, and prevent abuse;
  • Process subscriptions, trials, billing, and plan limits;
  • Communicate service updates, security notices, and support responses;
  • Comply with law, enforce our Terms, and protect NuMeAi, users, merchants, and third parties.

4. AI Processing

NuMeAi uses artificial intelligence and machine learning providers, including large language model and image-generation providers, to power features such as product recommendations, chat responses, market intelligence reports, campaign copy, and creative generation.

Information you submit to AI features may be sent to our AI service providers for processing. We seek to use business-grade providers and configurations that support confidentiality, security, and appropriate data handling. AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or not suitable for every business or legal situation. You should review AI-generated content before relying on it, publishing it, or using it in advertising.

Unless we separately notify you and obtain any required rights or permissions, we do not use your confidential business information to train NuMeAi models for unrelated third-party customers. We may use aggregated, de-identified, or statistical information to improve the Services.

When you connect third-party platforms, such as Shopify, Meta/Facebook/Instagram, TikTok, or other social, advertising, ecommerce, analytics, or publishing services, you authorize NuMeAi to access and process information made available through those integrations according to the permissions you grant.

Social and advertising platforms may impose their own terms, developer policies, content rules, privacy requirements, approval requirements, rate limits, and posting or advertising restrictions. Your use of connected platforms remains subject to those third-party terms.

Some posting flows may not be fully automated. For example, TikTok or other platforms may require creator approval, app review, available scopes, draft completion, or final action inside the third-party app before content appears publicly.
